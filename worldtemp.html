Are global measles trends moving in the right direction?

Progress in measles eradication across countries and continents

Over the past 4 decades progress in measles eradication has been uneven across the world, but overall there has been a significant declined in measles incidence. Nonetheless, the goal of measles elmination in five regions by 2020 set out in the Global Vaccine Action Plan is unlikely to be achieved, not least to due a surge in measles cases in 2019.
