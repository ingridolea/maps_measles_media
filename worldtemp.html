Are global measles trends moving in the right direction?

Progress in measles eradication across countries and continents

Over the past 4 decades progress in measles eradication has been uneven across the world, but overall there has been a significant declined in measles incidence. However,

Worldwide progress has been uneven, with notable upticks in cases in 2019

You’ll notice that while the number of cases in the US has increased in recent years, other countries have experienced a variety of trends. You can sort this chart by country, increase in cases, total number of cases and continent, or search for an individual country that you’re interested in.
