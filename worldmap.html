Measles in the world in 2019

Measles outbreaks

Sum of measles cases in 2019, by country and continent

In 2019 there were a total of 429,650 reported cases of measles worldwide. This visualization illustrates the global distribution of these cases. Country blocks are organized by continent and each country includes the total number of cases reported in that country from January 1 - December 31, 2019.

Cases of measles from WHO, get the data here. Visualization adapted from D3 Source Treemap by Mike Bostock

Prevalence of measles

Cases by 100,000 people

When considering the number of measles cases per country, it is important to consider the population of that country. The following visualization illustrates the number of measles cases per 100,000 people for the same countries in 2019.
